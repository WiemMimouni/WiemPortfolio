    WeeLance

    • Oversaw project development by managing a team of developers, assigning tasks, and facilitating effective collaboration to meet project goals and deadlines.

    • Developed real-time chat functionality using Socket.IO, enabling instant communication between users, fostering engagement, and enhancing the application's overall user experience.

    Trust-IT

    a responsive web application for a specialized phone repair company that streamlines the phone submission process for individuals and offers bonus features catering to enterprise and large-scale corporate clients.

    • Orchestrated the Agile development process, facilitating daily stand-up meetings, sprint planning, and retrospectives to ensure efficient project management and collaboration.
    • Acctively participated in the UX/UI design process using Figma, collaborating closely with designers to create a user-friendly and visually appealing interface and provided feedback and insights to ensure that the design aligns with user expectations and industry best practices.
    • Developed bonus features tailored to enterprise and large corporate clients, such as bulk phone submissions, custom repair tracking, and dedicated support channels.
    • Created comprehensive documentation for the application's features and functionalities, ensuring that team members and end-users have access to clear instructions.

    Ortho-smiling

    Led as a Product Owner, Scrum Master, and Developer for an orthopedic and prosthetic product company. Developed a comprehensive web application that introduced the company, streamlined quote requests through a user-friendly form, and integrated an e-commerce section for online product purchases.     • As a Product Owner, defined the product strategy and roadmap, aligning it with the company's goals and customer needs and prioritized features and functionalities, ensuring that the web application effectively introduced the company and provided a seamless quote request process.
    • Acted as the Scrum Master, orchestrating the Agile development process for the project and conducted sprint planning, daily stand-up meetings, and retrospectives to maintain an efficient and collaborative development workflow.
    • Led the development efforts to create a web application that effectively showcased the orthopedic and prosthetic products.
    • Implemented a user-friendly form for customers to request quotes, ensuring that it streamlined the inquiry process.
